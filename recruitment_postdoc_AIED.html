Postdoctoral Researcher Position in AIED, LA, or EDM at University of Florida


  1. Position Details


    2. About the Position Dr. Wanli Xing is seeking a highly motivated and dedicated postdoctoral researcher to join the AICE lab at University of Florida. The selected candidate will have rich opportunities for research, development, and grant proposals. Specifically, the candidate will work on the state-of-the-art AI technologies such as large language models in educational contexts, analyze large-scale educational data using theory-informed computing models, and disseminate findings through top-tiered conferences and journals in education. We will also work on grant proposals to seek federal, foundational, and internal support, with the possibility of the candidate taking a key personnel role.


    Location : Gainesville, Florida, US


    Duration : 2 years, with the possibility of extension


    Start Date : Negotiable


    Application Deadline : As soon as filled


    Qualifications:


    • PhD in educational technology, human-computer interaction, or computer science with a focus on AIED, LA, or EDM.
    • Proven track record of publications in reputed conferences and journals.
    • Proficiency in Python or other data science toolkits.
    • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
    • Preferred but not required: Knowledge of contemporary issues and trends in education and learning technology.
    • Preferred but not required: Knowledge of PyTorch, TensorFlow, LangChain, DeepSpeed (or similar optimization library), CUDA, and Slurm.

    Key responsibilities:


    • Conduct high-quality research and publish findings in reputable conferences and journals.
    • Collaborate on designing and developing AI-enabled technologies for STEM learning with a multidisciplinary team of researchers.
    • Assist in writing research proposals and securing funding for future projects.
    • Present research findings at conferences and workshops.

    Benefits:


    • Competitive salary and benefits package.
    • Access to state-of-the-art research facilities (e.g., HiPerGator with thousands of A100s).
    • Access to a vast network of researchers and professionals in the AIED, learning engineering, learning analytics, and educational data mining.
    • Opportunities for professional development and networking.

    How to Apply? Interested candidates should send your CV and a cover letter detailing your research interests and experience to wanli.xing@coe.ufl.edu. 2-3 reference letters will be sought for final candidates.



  2. Why University of Florida?


    • Overall Ranking: #6 among public universities; #28 among all universities (US News & World Report)
    • College of Education Ranking: #18 among all universities (US News & World Report)
    • Our PhD graduates regularly got tenure-track assistant professor and other competitive positions.
    • Location: UF is located in Gainesville, one of the most livable cities in the US and close to major themes parks (e.g., Disney World, Universal Studio) and beautiful Florida beaches and springs.
